## Frequently Asked Questions

### What's included in the free trial?

Full access to every feature in your chosen plan for 14 days. No credit card required. No feature restrictions. Import your existing inventory, test the billing, try scheme tracking — everything works.

### What happens after the trial ends?

Your data is preserved for 30 days. Choose a billing plan to continue, or export your data. No pressure, no auto-charge.

### Can I switch plans later?

Yes. Upgrade from General Trade to Multi-Store Chain at any time — your data carries over seamlessly. Contact us for downgrades.

### What payment methods do you accept?

UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking, and NEFT/RTGS for annual billing. GST invoice provided on every payment.

### Is there a setup fee or onboarding cost?

No setup fee for General Trade or Multi-Store Chain plans. We provide free onboarding assistance including data migration help and a guided walkthrough. Enterprise plans include dedicated onboarding at no extra cost.

### How long does setup take?

Most single-store retailers are live within 2 hours. Multi-store chains with existing data typically take 1–2 days for full migration and training.

### Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No lock-in contracts. Cancel before your next billing cycle and you won't be charged again. Your data is available for export for 30 days after cancellation.

### Is my data secure?

RetailerOS uses bank-grade encryption (AES-256) for data at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit. Your data is hosted on AWS India (Mumbai region) for compliance with Indian data localization requirements. Regular security audits and SOC 2 compliance.

### Do I need special hardware?

No. RetailerOS runs in your browser on any device — desktop, tablet, or phone. Use your existing barcode scanner or just your phone camera for IMEI capture.

### Do you support all brands?

We auto-load schemes from Samsung, Vivo, Oppo, Xiaomi, OnePlus, Realme, Apple, Motorola, Nothing, and more. New brands are added regularly. If your brand isn't listed, we'll add it within 48 hours.

### What if I have more than 15 stores?

Contact our sales team for custom Enterprise pricing with volume discounts, dedicated support, and SLA guarantees.

### Can RetailerOS integrate with my existing accounting software?

The Multi-Store Chain plan includes API access for custom integrations. We support direct export to Tally and other popular accounting tools. General Trade plan supports manual data export.

## CTA Strategy & Recommendations

### Primary CTA: "Start Free Trial"

**Rationale:** "Start Free Trial" outperforms "Book a Demo" for self-serve SaaS products targeting SMBs. Indian telecom retailers are hands-on operators — they want to try the software themselves, not sit through a demo call. The 14-day trial with no credit card requirement removes all friction.

- **Placement:** Hero section, each pricing tier card, sticky header/footer on scroll
- **Design:** High-contrast button (e.g., white text on brand primary color), prominent sizing
- **Copy variants to A/B test:**
  - "Start Free Trial — No Credit Card"
  - "Try RetailerOS Free for 14 Days"
  - "Get Started Free →"

### Secondary CTA: "Talk to Sales"

**Rationale:** Enterprise buyers (15+ stores) need custom quotes and want human reassurance before committing. This CTA serves chains and distributors who want negotiated pricing.

- **Placement:** Enterprise tier card, bottom of pricing page, FAQ section
- **Copy:** "Talk to Sales" or "Get Custom Pricing"
