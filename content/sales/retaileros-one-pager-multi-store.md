## The Challenge

You opened more stores to grow revenue. But more stores also means more leakage:

- **Stock discrepancies between locations** — nobody knows which store actually has the device a customer wants.
- **Inconsistent scheme claims** — Store A claims the Samsung cashback. Store B misses it entirely. Same chain, different margins.
- **No centralized reporting** — you are stitching together Tally exports and WhatsApp messages to understand your own business.
- **Compliance risk multiplied** — Sanchar Saathi IMEI compliance and GST e-invoicing must work correctly at every location. One store's error is your entire chain's liability.

Without proper systems, a typical ₹50 lakh/month store loses ₹2.55-5 lakh every month to shrinkage, missed payouts, and operational waste. **Multiply that across five stores: ₹12-25 lakh per month walking out the door.**

## One Dashboard, Every Store

RetailerOS gives chain operators what enterprise retailers have always had — without the enterprise price tag or six-month implementation.

**Centralized Real-Time Dashboard**
See sales, inventory levels, and staff activity across every location from a single screen. No more calling store managers for updates.

**Per-Store Analytics**
Drill into any store: top-selling SKUs, staff performance, scheme claim rates, and dead stock levels — compared across your chain so you know exactly where to intervene.

**Inter-Store Transfer Tracking**
Every IMEI is tracked from warehouse to store to store to customer. Transfer a device between locations and the audit trail follows it. Shrinkage drops below 0.5%.

**Centralized Scheme Management**
Brand schemes from Samsung, Vivo, Oppo, Xiaomi — loaded once, applied automatically across all stores at billing. No more missed deadlines or inconsistent claims.

**Role-Based Staff Management**
Define what each employee sees and does. Store managers see their location. Regional leads see their cluster. You see everything. Per-employee sales and performance tracking included.

**GST-Compliant Billing with E-Invoicing**
Correct HSN codes, tax breakdowns, and e-invoicing at every location by default. One reconciliation flow for your entire chain.
