## The Problem: Your Margins Are Leaking

If you run an independent mobile or electronics shop doing ₹50 lakh+ per month, you are losing ₹1.5-3 lakh every month to problems that don't show up in Tally:

- **IMEI chaos** -- 3-5% inventory shrinkage from untracked serial numbers. Five missing handsets at ₹15,000 each is ₹75,000 gone in a single month.
- **Missed scheme payouts** -- Samsung, Vivo, Oppo schemes expire unclaimed. Manual tracking captures only 60-70% of eligible payouts. That is 10-15% revenue leakage.
- **GST headaches** -- Wrong HSN codes, missing invoices, failed reconciliations. Hours lost every week, plus penalty risk on every audit.
- **Sanchar Saathi compliance** -- IMEI registration mandates are tightening. Without serial-level tracking, you face regulatory risk on every device you sell.

**The cost of doing nothing: ₹18-36 lakh per year walking out the door.**

---

## The Solution: One Platform, Every Problem Solved

**IMEI Tracking** -- Scan-to-track every device from purchase to sale. Barcode, camera, or manual entry. Full audit trail. Shrinkage drops below 0.5%.

**Scheme Management** -- Auto-loads brand schemes, applies them at billing, alerts you before expiry. Claim rates improve from 60-70% to 85-95%.

**GST-Compliant Billing** -- Every invoice is correct by default. HSN codes, tax breakdowns, e-invoicing support. Zero penalty risk.

**Inventory Management** -- Real-time stock visibility across locations. Demand-driven reorder alerts. Dead stock drops from 8-12% to 3-5%.

**Sanchar Saathi Ready** -- Serial-level IMEI records for every transaction. Regulatory compliance built into your daily workflow, not bolted on after the fact.
