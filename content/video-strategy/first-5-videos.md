# First 5 Videos — Content Plan

*Khosha Systems | March 2026*

These are the first 5 videos Khosha Systems should produce, ordered by impact and ease of production. Each includes the concept, target audience, production approach, tools, and estimated effort.

## Video 1: RetailerOS — 90-Second Product Explainer

**Concept:** "Stop losing money on billing errors. RetailerOS manages your telecom store — billing, IMEI tracking, and scheme management — in one place."

**Target audience:** Telecom retail store owners and managers in India (top of funnel)

**Format:** AI avatar presenter (30s) + screen recording of actual UI (45s) + CTA (15s)

**Script outline:**
1. **Hook (0-5s):** "Running a telecom store with spreadsheets? Here's what you're missing."
2. **Problem (5-20s):** AI avatar describes common pain points — billing disputes, IMEI mismatches, scheme reconciliation headaches
3. **Solution (20-65s):** Screen recording walkthrough showing: creating a bill → IMEI auto-tracking → scheme application → dashboard view
4. **Social proof (65-75s):** Quick stat or testimonial quote overlay
5. **CTA (75-90s):** Avatar returns — "Try RetailerOS free. Link in description."

**Tools:**
- HeyGen — AI avatar presenter segments
- OBS Studio — screen recording of RetailerOS UI
- ffmpeg — stitch segments, add subtitles (English + Hindi), watermark, export YouTube (16:9) + LinkedIn (4:5) + Reels (9:16)

**Distribution:** Website homepage, YouTube, LinkedIn, Instagram Reels

**Estimated effort:** 1 day (script + recording + assembly)

**Cost:** ~$0 incremental (HeyGen subscription covers it)

## Video 2: "Why Most Telecom Retailers Still Lose Money on Schemes" — Founder Thought Leadership

**Concept:** Short, direct-to-camera style video where the founder/team member shares an industry insight about telecom retail scheme management challenges.

**Target audience:** Telecom retail owners, industry professionals on LinkedIn

**Format:** 60-second talking head (founder or AI avatar) with text overlays

**Script outline:**
1. **Hook (0-5s):** "I've seen stores leave ₹2-3 lakhs on the table every month. Here's why."
2. **Insight (5-40s):** Explain the scheme reconciliation problem — carriers offer complex rebate schemes, most stores track them manually, discrepancies go unnoticed
3. **Pivot (40-50s):** "We built RetailerOS to solve exactly this. It auto-tracks every scheme and flags mismatches."
4. **CTA (50-60s):** "Follow for more telecom retail tips. Link to try RetailerOS in bio."

**Tools:**
- Option A (preferred): Smartphone recording of actual founder — more authentic for LinkedIn
- Option B: HeyGen AI avatar if founder isn't available
- ffmpeg — add captions (critical for LinkedIn autoplay), brand lower-third, export for LinkedIn (4:5)

**Distribution:** LinkedIn (primary — native upload, not YouTube link), Instagram Reels (repurposed)

**Estimated effort:** 2-3 hours

**Cost:** ~$0

**Target audience:** Real estate brokers, agents, and property developers in India

**Format:** Screen recording with voiceover narration + text annotations

**Distribution:** YouTube (primary — SEO for "real estate CRM India"), website product page, LinkedIn

**Estimated effort:** 1 day

**Cost:** ~$0 incremental

**Target audience:** Office managers, facility managers, corporate real estate teams, co-working spaces in India

- ffmpeg — assembly, subtitles, multi-format

**Distribution:** YouTube, website VMS product page, LinkedIn

**Estimated effort:** 1 day

**Cost:** ~$0 incremental

## Video 5: Customer Testimonial — RetailerOS Success Story

**Concept:** A real RetailerOS customer describes how the software solved their billing/scheme management problems, with specific metrics.

**Target audience:** Prospective RetailerOS customers in evaluation stage (bottom of funnel)

**Format:** 90-120 seconds, interview-style with B-roll of the customer's store

**Script framework (customer speaks):**
1. **Who they are (0-15s):** "I'm [Name], I run [X] telecom stores in [City]."
2. **Problem before (15-35s):** "We were doing billing in [old way]. Every month we'd find discrepancies worth ₹[X]."
3. **Why they switched (35-50s):** "We tried RetailerOS because [reason]."
4. **Results (50-80s):** "In 3 months, billing errors dropped by [X]%. Scheme reconciliation is automatic now. We save [X] hours per week."
5. **Recommendation (80-100s):** "If you're running a telecom store, you need this."

**Tools:**
- Smartphone + clip-on lavalier mic — interview recording at customer's store
- B-roll: 30 seconds of store footage, product on screen, team using the app
- DaVinci Resolve or CapCut — editing (this one benefits from human editing touch)
- ffmpeg — subtitles (English + Hindi), branded intro/outro, multi-format export

**Distribution:** Website testimonials section, YouTube, LinkedIn, sales team shares directly with prospects

**Estimated effort:** 1 day for filming + 1 day for editing = 2 days

**Cost:** ~₹5,000-15,000 (travel to customer location + basic equipment if needed)
