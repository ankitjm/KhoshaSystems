## 8. FAQ Content (Google Business Q&A)

### Q1: What industries does Khosha Systems work with?
Our flagship products serve telecom retail (RetailerOS), real estate (Real Estate CRM), and corporate facility management (VMS). For custom projects, we work with any industry where software can solve a real operational problem.

### Q2: Do you build custom software or only sell your existing products?
Both. We have our own products — RetailerOS, Real Estate CRM, and Visitor Management System — and we also build fully custom software for businesses. Custom projects include web apps, mobile apps, SaaS platforms, and AI integrations. Every project is built from scratch, not templated.

### Q3: How long does a typical project take?
It depends on scope. A landing page takes 1-2 weeks. A full web application takes 2-4 months. A complex SaaS product can take 4-8 months. We give honest timelines upfront and do not overpromise. We will tell you if your timeline is unrealistic.

### Q4: Do you work with startups or only established companies?
We work with both. For startups, we help with MVP development, architecture planning, and scaling. For established companies, we handle digital transformation, legacy system replacement, and new product development. The requirement is that you have a clear problem to solve and are serious about building.

### Q5: What is your pricing model?
We offer project-based pricing for defined scope work and dedicated team models for ongoing development. We do not do hourly billing — it incentivizes the wrong things. Pricing depends on project complexity. Reach out through khoshasystems.com for a free consultation.

### Q6: Can you help with AI integration for my existing business?
Yes. Our AI Transformation service is designed for exactly this. We start by identifying where AI will actually deliver ROI in your business — not everywhere, but in the right places. Common use cases include demand forecasting, lead scoring, document processing, and customer support automation. We build production-ready solutions, not proof-of-concept demos.

### Q7: Are your products built for Indian businesses specifically?
Yes. Our products handle GST compliance, integrate with Indian platforms (99acres, MagicBricks, IndiaMART), support Indian payment systems, and are designed for Indian network and infrastructure conditions. This is not a foreign product localized for India — it is built here, for here.

### Q8: Where is your office located?
We are based in Indiranagar, Bengaluru, Karnataka. We work with clients across India and can operate remotely. For local clients, in-person meetings are available by appointment.

## Review Management Notes

While not a content section, these guidelines help maximize the profile:

- **Review request timing:** Ask for reviews 1-2 weeks after project delivery or product onboarding
- **Response template for positive reviews:** Thank the reviewer by name, reference the specific project or product, mention one result achieved
- **Response template for negative reviews:** Acknowledge the issue, provide a direct contact (email/phone) for resolution, do not argue publicly
- **Review velocity target:** 2-4 new reviews per month
- **Do not:** Offer incentives for reviews (violates Google policy), use fake reviews, ignore negative feedback
