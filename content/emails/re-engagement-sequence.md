# Re-Engagement Sequence — 3-Email Series (Cold Leads)

**Trigger:** No email opens, clicks, or website visits for 30+ days after completing the welcome sequence (or after last activity).
**Cadence:** Day 0 (trigger), Day 5, Day 12
**From:** Ankit Mehta, Founder & Chief Architect — Khosha Systems <hello@khoshasystems.com>
**Goal:** Re-activate interest or gracefully sunset the lead. Leads that don't engage after this sequence are moved to a low-priority "Archive" list.
**Personalization:** Four vertical variants — RetailerOS, Real Estate CRM, VMS, General.

## Implementation Notes

### Brevo Automation Setup — Re-Engagement Sequence

**Automation name:** `Re-Engagement — [Vertical]`

**Entry trigger:**
- Contact is on "Warm Leads" list (moved there after completing welcome sequence without converting)
- AND contact attribute `last_activity_date` is more than 30 days ago
- AND contact attribute `demo_booked` = false
- AND contact attribute `unsubscribed` = false

**Entry filter (important):**
- Exclude contacts who booked a demo at any point
- Exclude contacts who replied to any email in the past 30 days
- Exclude contacts on the "Customer" list (already converted)

**Step-by-step Brevo flow:**
1. **Step 1:** Update attribute `reengagement_stage` = 1
2. **Step 2:** Send Email 1 (Value Check-In + What's New)
3. **Step 3:** Wait 5 days
4. **Step 4:** Condition — Has contact opened Email 1 or visited website?
   - Yes → Send Email 2 (Comparison) with "engaged" subject line variant
   - No → Send Email 2 (Comparison) with standard subject line
5. **Step 5:** Wait 7 days
6. **Step 6:** Condition — Has contact engaged with any re-engagement email?
   - Yes → Send Email 3 (Sunset) — these leads are engaged but not converting, sunset gracefully
   - No → Send Email 3 (Sunset) — truly cold, sunset and archive
7. **Step 7:** Wait 14 days
8. **Step 8:** Condition — Did contact reply with "later", "demo", or "talk"?
   - "demo"/"talk" → Move to "Demo Requested" list, notify sales
   - "later" → Move to "Nurture — 3 Month Check-In" list
   - No reply → Move to "Archived Leads" list (do not delete — may reactivate via future campaigns)

**Reply detection:**
- Brevo can detect replies via the "Contact replied" condition in automations
- For keyword detection ("demo", "later"), use Brevo's webhook + a simple serverless function to parse reply content and update contact attributes
- Alternatively: use reply-to routing to hello@khoshasystems.com and manually triage (simpler for low volume)

### Contact Attributes to Add
- `reengagement_stage` (number): 1-3
- `last_activity_date` (date): auto-updated on email open, click, or website visit
- `reengagement_reply` (text): "demo", "later", "not_interested", or blank
- `reengagement_completed` (boolean): set true when sequence finishes

### Success Metrics

| Email | Open Rate Target | Click Rate Target | Key Metric |
|-------|-----------------|-------------------|------------|
| Email 1 (What's New) | >20% | >3% | Reactivation rate (any engagement) |
| Email 2 (Comparison) | >18% | >4% | Product page / comparison page visits |
| Email 3 (Sunset) | >25% | — | Reply rate ("demo"/"later" responses) |
| **Sequence total** | — | — | **>8% reactivation rate, >1% demo booking** |

### Notes on Email 3 (Sunset)
- Higher open rates expected because subject line creates curiosity/urgency
- The 3-option reply format is intentional — it gives leads an easy, low-commitment way to stay connected
- "Reply later" responses are valuable — they're permission to re-engage in 3 months
- Leads who don't respond should be archived, not deleted — they may come back via paid campaigns or referrals
