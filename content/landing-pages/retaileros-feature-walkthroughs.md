## Inventory Management & IMEI Tracking

**You have 4,000 IMEIs across three stores. Do you know exactly where each one is right now?**

### The Problem

Indian telecom and electronics retailers deal with a unique headache that most retail software ignores entirely: IMEI-level tracking. Every phone, every tablet, every smartwatch has a unique serial number that needs to be tracked from the moment it arrives in your store to the moment a customer walks out with it.

Most retailers manage this with Excel sheets, paper registers, or some combination of both. The result is predictable. Stock goes missing and nobody knows when it happened. A customer brings in a warranty claim and you spend 20 minutes hunting for the purchase record. Your distributor disputes a return because you cannot prove which specific unit you sent back. Inter-store transfers turn into a black hole — you sent 15 phones from Store A to Store B, but Store B says they received 14. Who is right? Nobody knows.

Industry data shows that Indian electronics retailers lose 3-5% of inventory value to shrinkage every year. For a store doing ₹50 lakh in monthly revenue, that is ₹1.5-2.5 lakh vanishing every month. That is not a rounding error. That is your profit margin.

### How RetailerOS Solves It

RetailerOS tracks every IMEI from the second it enters your ecosystem. When stock arrives from a distributor, your staff scans each device — barcode scanner, phone camera, or manual entry. The system creates a permanent record tied to that specific unit. From that point forward, every movement is logged.

When you transfer stock between stores, RetailerOS tracks it at the serial level. Store A marks 15 devices as "transferred out." Store B scans them in. If only 14 arrive, the system flags the discrepancy immediately — not three weeks later during a manual count.

At the point of sale, the IMEI is automatically captured during billing. No separate step, no extra effort. The device moves from "in stock" to "sold" with a complete chain of custody that links back to the original purchase, the distributor, the transfer history, and the final customer.

The audit trail is permanent and tamper-proof. If a distributor questions a return, you pull up the record in seconds. If a customer disputes a warranty date, you have the data. If the GST department wants to see your stock movement records, you print the report.

## POS & GST-Compliant Billing

**"GST notice" — two words that keep Indian retailers up at night. They do not have to.**

### The Problem

Indian retail billing is not simple. It was never simple, and GST made it more complicated. Every invoice needs the correct HSN code. Tax breakdowns must show CGST, SGST, or IGST depending on whether the sale is intra-state or inter-state. Reverse charge mechanism applies to certain transactions. Returns require proper credit notes — not just a crossed-out entry in a register. And now, e-invoicing is mandatory for businesses above the threshold.

Most retail billing software treats GST compliance as an afterthought — a checkbox feature bolted onto a system designed for a different market. The result: retailers spend hours fixing invoices, manually looking up HSN codes, and praying that their returns are documented correctly. One retailer in Hyderabad told us he spends 6 hours every week just fixing billing errors before filing GST returns. That is 6 hours he is not spending on selling.

Then there is the checkout speed problem. In a busy telecom store during a Diwali sale or a new phone launch, every second at the counter matters. If your billing staff has to manually type an IMEI, look up the HSN code, calculate the tax, and format the invoice — you are losing customers to the shop next door.

### How RetailerOS Solves It

RetailerOS was built for Indian tax compliance from the ground up — not retrofitted. Every product in your catalog is mapped to its correct HSN code. Tax calculations happen automatically based on the transaction type and location. When your staff creates an invoice, it is GST-compliant by default, not by effort.

The IMEI auto-capture feature cuts checkout time by 40%. Scan the device, and RetailerOS pulls the product details, applies the correct pricing and tax, and generates a compliant invoice. Your staff does not need to memorize HSN codes or tax rates. The system handles it.

Returns and exchanges follow proper credit note workflows. When a customer brings back a device, RetailerOS generates the credit note with the correct references to the original invoice, adjusts your GST liability, and updates your inventory — all in one flow. No manual reconciliation needed.

E-invoicing integration means you generate IRN-compliant invoices directly from your billing screen. No separate portal, no copy-pasting, no formatting headaches. For retailers above the e-invoicing threshold, this alone saves hours every month.

- Native HSN code mapping for every telecom and electronics SKU — no manual lookups
- Automatic CGST/SGST/IGST calculation based on transaction type and location
- Reverse charge mechanism handled automatically for applicable transactions
