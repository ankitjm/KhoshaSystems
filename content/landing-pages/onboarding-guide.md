## Step 1: Account Setup

**Time needed:** 10–15 minutes

Getting started is simple. Here is what to do:

1. **Sign up** at [retaileros.in](https://retaileros.in) using your mobile number or email.
2. **Add your business details** — business name, address, phone number, and email.
3. **Enter your GST number** — RetailerOS will automatically pull your GST details, saving you time.
4. **Add your store locations** — running more than one store? Add all of them now. You can manage everything from one dashboard.

Once done, you will receive a confirmation on WhatsApp and email.

**Tip:** Keep your GST certificate handy. It makes this step faster.

Need help? Just message our support team on WhatsApp and they will walk you through it.

---

## Step 2: Import Your Data

**Time needed:** 15–30 minutes (depending on data size)

No need to start from scratch. Bring your existing data into RetailerOS easily:

- **Inventory** — Upload your stock list using a CSV or Excel file. We support bulk IMEI imports too.
- **Customer data** — Import your customer list so you never lose track of relationships.
- **Supplier list** — Add your distributors and suppliers for smoother purchase orders.

**Do not worry about formatting.** Our support team will help you prepare and upload your files. Just share what you have — even a handwritten register photo works. We will handle the rest.

**Tip:** If you are moving from another software, let us know. We have helped hundreds of stores migrate smoothly.

---

## Step 3: Configure Billing

**Time needed:** 10–15 minutes

Set up your billing so every invoice is GST-compliant from day one:

1. **Verify GST details** — Confirm your GSTIN, business type, and tax preferences.
2. **Add HSN codes** — RetailerOS comes with pre-loaded HSN codes for common telecom and electronics products. Just select the right ones.
3. **Choose your invoice template** — Pick a professional template that matches your brand. Add your logo and store details.
4. **Set up payment methods** — Enable cash, UPI, card, and credit payment tracking.

Every invoice you generate will be GST-ready, with proper tax breakdowns and formatting.

**Tip:** Preview a sample invoice before your first sale. Make sure your store name, address, and GST number look correct.

---

## Step 4: Set Up Inventory

**Time needed:** 20–30 minutes

This is where RetailerOS truly shines. Organize your stock like never before:

1. **Scan IMEIs** — Use your phone camera or a barcode scanner to add devices. Each IMEI is tracked from purchase to sale.
2. **Organize by brand and category** — Group products by brand (Samsung, Vivo, Xiaomi, etc.) and category (smartphones, accessories, chargers).
3. **Set reorder points** — Tell RetailerOS when stock is running low. You will get automatic alerts so you never miss a sale due to stockouts.
4. **Review your dashboard** — Check that all imported and scanned items show up correctly.

**Tip:** Start with your best-selling products. You can always add more items later. Do not try to do everything on day one.

---

## Step 5: Add Your Team

**Time needed:** 10 minutes

Get your staff on board so everyone can work together:

1. **Invite team members** — Add staff by entering their mobile number or email. They will get a link to join.
2. **Set roles and permissions** — Choose who can do what:
   - **Sales staff** — can create bills and view inventory
   - **Store manager** — can manage stock, returns, and reports
   - **Owner** — full access to everything including finances
3. **Quick training** — Have each team member do one practice billing transaction. It takes just 5 minutes to learn.

RetailerOS is designed to be simple, so your team will pick it up fast — even if they are not used to software.

**Tip:** Our support team can do a free 15-minute training call with your staff. Just ask.

---

## Step 6: Connect Brand Schemes

**Time needed:** 5–10 minutes

Stop tracking brand schemes on paper. Let RetailerOS do it for you:

1. **Browse available schemes** — See active schemes from brands like Samsung, Vivo, Oppo, Xiaomi, Realme, and more.
2. **Activate the ones relevant to you** — Select the brands and schemes that apply to your store.
3. **Track automatically** — Every eligible sale is matched to the right scheme. No more missed claims or manual calculations.
4. **View your earnings** — Check scheme earnings in real time from your dashboard.

This feature alone saves most store owners hours of work every month.

**Tip:** Review your scheme dashboard weekly to ensure no claims are pending. RetailerOS will remind you, but a quick check never hurts.

---

## Step 7: Go Live

**Time needed:** 5 minutes for your first sale

You are ready. Here is how to make your first sale on RetailerOS:

1. **Open the billing screen** and search for the product or scan the IMEI.
2. **Add the customer** — search existing or add a new one.
3. **Apply any discounts or schemes** if applicable.
4. **Select payment method** and confirm.
5. **Generate the invoice** — share it via print, WhatsApp, or SMS.

**Your first-day checklist:**
- [ ] Complete one real sale on RetailerOS
- [ ] Print or share an invoice with a customer
- [ ] Check that the sale appears in your dashboard
- [ ] Ask your team to complete one sale each

**Tip:** Keep your old billing method available for the first day, just in case. By day two, you will not need it.

## Pro Tips — Get the Most Out of RetailerOS in Your First 30 Days

**1. Use the mobile app daily.**
RetailerOS works on your phone browser. Bookmark it on your home screen so you can check sales, stock, and alerts even when you are away from the store.

**2. Review your dashboard every morning.**
Spend 2 minutes each morning checking yesterday's sales, low-stock alerts, and pending scheme claims. This small habit gives you full control over your business.

**3. Collect customer phone numbers with every sale.**
The more customer data you have, the better RetailerOS can help you with repeat business, warranty tracking, and targeted promotions.

**4. Set up reorder alerts for your top 20 products.**
Stockouts cost you money. Let RetailerOS alert you before you run out, so you can reorder on time and never lose a sale.

**5. Explore the analytics section after your first week.**
Once you have a few days of data, check out the analytics dashboard. You will discover which products sell fastest, which days are busiest, and where your margins are strongest. These insights help you make smarter buying decisions.
