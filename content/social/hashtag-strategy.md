## Platform-Specific Guidelines

### LinkedIn
- Use 3–5 hashtags per post (algorithm penalizes more)
- Place hashtags at the end of the post
- Mix 1 broad + 2 mid-range + 1–2 niche per post
- Always include #KhoshaSystems

### Twitter/X
- Use 2–3 hashtags per tweet (more hurts engagement)
- Integrate naturally into tweet text when possible
- Use trending hashtags when relevant to our content
- Always include #KhoshaSystems on product tweets

## Hashtag Combinations by Post Type

**Product announcement:**
#KhoshaSystems #RetailTech #BuiltForIndia + 1 product niche tag
